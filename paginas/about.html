About Us

Prime Build Works is more than just changing a kitchen countertop; it's about transforming spaces so it tells stories. Located in Calgary, Alberta, we were founded on the conviction that quality and transparency must go hand-in-hand with every remodeling project.

We offer a unique combined service of high-quality remodeling and controlled material distribution. As local experts, we are familiar with the specific Canadian design trends and regulations, and we are committed to energy efficiency and sustainability.
