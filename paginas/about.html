About Us

Prime Build Works is more than just changing a kitchen countertop; it's about transforming spaces so it tells stories. Located in Calgary, Alberta , we were founded on the conviction that quality and transparency must go hand-in-hand with every remodeling project.
We offer a unique combined service of high-quality remodeling and controlled material distribution. As local experts, we are familiar with the specific Canadian design trends and regulations, and we are committed to energy efficiency and sustainability.

Service

Kitchen and Bathroom Countertops

We specialize in the fabrication and flawless installation of kitchen countertops. We transform the heart of your home with high-quality materials, ensuring precision in every cut and a finish that revalues your property.

Premium Material Distribution

We provide high-quality materials for your remodeling project. Thanks to our partnerships with the principal granite suppliers in Calgary and beyond (such as Mondial, GS, Hari Stone, Caesar Stone, and TCE), we can offer you an exclusive selection of natural and synthetic stone, ensuring quality right from the source.

Complete Remodeling

If you are looking for a complete renovation of your kitchen or bathroom, we're your trusted partner every step of the way, applying our knowledge of Canadian design trends.
